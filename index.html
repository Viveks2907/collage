About S.S.T College of Arts & Commerce

(Affiliated to the University of Mumbai, Mumbai)

SST College of Arts & Commerce is a premier educational institution in Mumbai, Maharashtra established with an aim to equip the youth with quality as well as career oriented education based on high ethical values enabling them to achieve their potential and creative abilities. We empower our students to become responsible and productive members of the society.
